Travel together, safely.

This app helps solo travelers find people going on the same route and chat after matching. It’s made to feel easy and friendly — like using a simple game menu.

Tip: Click Start (Demo) and fill the form on the right 👉

How it works (super simple)

3 steps • 1 minute
1

Tell your trip

From, To, and when you’re traveling. That’s it.

2

See matches

The app shows people going on the same route.

3

Chat safely

Say hi, ask simple questions, and decide to connect.
